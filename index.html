About me

I'm a PhD student at the Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann (Grenoble Alpes University) in the ELAN team (inria). I'm under the supervision of Florence Bertails-Descoubes and Sébastien Neukirch.

I studied at the école normale supérieure de Lyon, I made an Erasmus semester at the ETH Zürich.
